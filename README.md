## Project Overview
This project is based on the concept of digital literacy, which is an important skill for students in today’s digital world. The main aim of this project is to help students understand how to use digital tools safely, effectively, and responsibly.

In this project, I completed five different tasks related to digital awareness from the given 5 module. First, I created an infographic to explain digital literacy and its importance. Then, I built my digital portfolio by creating profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. This helped me understand how to maintain a professional online presence.

I also explored coding and collaboration platforms by solving a basic and advanced problem on HackerRank and creating a Google Form quiz. In addition, I learned how to write professional emails and understood the importance of proper communication through email etiquette.

Finally, I studied cybercrime, especially phishing, and learned how such attacks happen and how we can prevent them. This project helped me improve my digital skills, awareness, and confidence in using online platforms. Overall, it was a useful and practical learning experience.

## Tasks

### Task 1 – Infographic
In this task, I designed a digital literacy awareness infographic using an online tool. The purpose of the infographic was to help students understand the importance of digital literacy in today’s world. I included key topics such as safe internet practices, useful digital tools for students, maintaining a professional online presence, and basic email etiquette. Through this task, I learned how to present information in a visually appealing and concise manner. It also helped me understand how design and communication play an important role in spreading awareness effectively. Creating the infographic improved my creativity and my ability to simplify complex concepts.

### Task 2 – Digital Portfolio
In this task, I created and updated my digital presence by setting up profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. I added essential academic details such as my course, institution, and interests. This task helped me understand the importance of building a professional online identity. I learned how these platforms are used by students and professionals to showcase their skills, collaborate, and explore opportunities. It also made me realize how maintaining a strong digital portfolio can help in internships and career growth. This task encouraged me to start building my profile early for future use.

### Task 3 – Platforms
In this task, I explored coding and collaboration tools. I created an account on HackerRank and solved a beginner-level as well as advance level problem, which helped me understand the basics of coding platforms. Additionally, I created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy awareness with different types of questions. I also viewed the responses through Google Sheets. This task helped me learn how online tools can be used for both learning and collaboration. It improved my understanding of digital tools that are useful in academics and group work.

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
In this task, I learned about professional communication by writing two formal emails. One email was addressed to a professor requesting an extension, and the other was for applying for an internship opportunity. I focused on maintaining a proper structure, polite tone, and clear subject lines. Additionally, I created a checklist of social media do’s and don’ts, which highlights responsible online behavior. This task helped me understand how important proper communication is in academic and professional environments.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied phishing as a form of cybercrime and wrote a detailed case study explaining how it occurs, who it targets, and its consequences. I also created a prevention checklist that includes practical steps to stay safe online, especially while using digital payments and social platforms. This task increased my awareness of online threats and the importance of cybersecurity. It also encouraged me to be more careful while sharing personal information online and to follow safe digital practices in daily life.
