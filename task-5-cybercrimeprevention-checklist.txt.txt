PREVENTION CHECKLIST
Stay Safe Online Checklist
* Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone
* Do not scan unknown QR codes
* Always verify links before clicking
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions
* Check sender details before trusting any message
* Do not respond to urgent or suspicious offers
* Regularly update apps and security settings
 UPI / Financial Safety 
* Always check payment requests before approving
* Use only official apps like bank apps or trusted platforms
Reporting Channels 
* National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
* Helpline Number: 1930
